Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgroSense?

AgroSense is a comprehensive smart farming platform that combines cutting-edge technology with agricultural expertise. We provide real-time monitoring, weather forecasting, pest detection, market intelligence, and AI-powered assistance to help farmers make data-driven decisions and increase productivity.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, enter your mobile number or email, verify your OTP, and complete your profile with basic farm details. You'll get instant access to our free tier features.

Is AgroSense available in my language?

Yes! AgroSense supports multiple Indian languages including Hindi, Punjabi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, and English. You can change your language preference anytime from the settings menu or the language selector in the navigation bar.

What devices are supported?

AgroSense works on smartphones (Android & iOS), tablets, and computers. You can access it through our mobile app or web browser. The platform is optimized for all screen sizes and works even with slow internet connections.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial with full access to all premium features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to continue with our free basic plan or upgrade to a premium plan for advanced features.

How accurate is the weather forecast?

Our weather predictions use advanced AI models combined with data from multiple meteorological sources, achieving 85-90% accuracy for 7-day forecasts. We provide hyperlocal predictions specific to your farm's GPS coordinates, updated every 6 hours.

How does the pest detection feature work?

Simply take a photo of the affected crop using your smartphone. Our AI analyzes the image to identify pests, diseases, or nutrient deficiencies within seconds. You'll receive detailed information about the problem and recommended treatments in your language.

Can I use AgroSense offline?

The mobile app offers limited offline functionality. You can view previously loaded data, access saved articles, and record farm activities offline. The data will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

How do I set up soil monitoring sensors?

If you have compatible IoT sensors, you can connect them via the "Add Device" option in settings. Follow the step-by-step pairing guide in the app. For sensors purchased from our partners, we offer free installation assistance.

What if the app crashes or has bugs?

First, try restarting the app and checking for updates. If the problem persists, report it through the "Report Bug" option in settings with a screenshot. Our technical team typically responds within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery (for hardware purchases). All transactions are secured with bank-grade encryption.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime without penalties. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your billing cycle. After that, you'll automatically switch to the free plan.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for premium subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact support within 7 days of purchase for a full refund. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes! We offer 20% discount on annual plans, special pricing for farmer cooperatives, and seasonal promotions. Students and small farmers may qualify for additional subsidies through our partnership with government schemes.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Change Plan. You can upgrade anytime (charges prorated). Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

How does the AI voice assistant work?

Click the microphone icon and speak your question in your preferred language. The AI understands natural language queries about farming practices, weather, pests, and more. It responds with voice answers and shows relevant information on screen.

Where does market price data come from?

We aggregate real-time data from government AGMARKNET portals, local mandis, and verified traders. Prices are updated multiple times daily and show trends to help you decide the best time to sell.

Can I track multiple farms?

Yes! Premium users can add unlimited farm locations. Each farm has its own dashboard with customized weather, soil data, and crop recommendations. Switch between farms easily from the menu.

How do I get crop recommendations?

Based on your location, soil type, season, and historical data, our AI suggests the best crops to grow. You'll see potential yield estimates, required inputs, and expected market prices for informed decision-making.

Can I share data with my agronomist?

Yes! You can invite agronomists, family members, or farm workers to view your data. Set different permission levels (view-only or full access) for each person from the sharing settings.
